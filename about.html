HACKATHON

Event Name: Tech Innovators Hackathon

When: October 15-16, 2023 (Saturday and Sunday)

Who Can Join:

  • Anyone 18 years or older can participate.
  • You don't need to be a tech expert; beginners are welcome.
  • You can be from anywhere in the world.

What's the Event About:

The Tech Innovators Hackathon is a gathering where people work together to come up with clever ideas and build tech solutions for real-world problems. It's a chance to learn, show your skills, and tackle important issues.

How you can take part:

  • You can join in person or online.
  • In-Person: You come to a place called the XYZ Innovation Center.
  • Online: You can join from your computer or wherever you are.

How to Register:

  • Go to the event's website: www.codechackathon.com.
  • Fill your info on registration page www.registerhackathon.com

What Happens After You Register:

Once you're in, you'll get an email with more details, like how to take part either in person or online.

How Your Work Will Be Judged:

  • You have until 5:00 PM on October 16, 2023, to finish your project.
  • Judges will look at:
    1. How unique and fresh your idea is
    2. How much your solution can help solve a real problem.
    3. How well your tech stuff works
    4. How good your presentation is.
    5. If you and your team work together well.

Prizes:

If your project is the best in your chosen area or the best overall, you can win cool stuff, like money, help from experts, and chances to work in exciting places.

When Winners Are Announced:

Winners will be announced at 6:30 PM on October 16, 2023. If you're not there in person, you can watch online.

Getting Along:

Everyone needs to be kind and respectful to each other. If you don't, you might have to leave the event
