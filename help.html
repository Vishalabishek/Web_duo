Help - Hackathon Registration

Hackathon Registration Instructions:

  1. Access the Registration Page:

    Open your web browser and visit the hackathon registration page. You can usually find the registration page by following a link or URL provided by the hackathon organizers.

    Click this URL to go to hackathon registration page www.registerhackathon.com after reading the following instructions.

  2. Navigate the Website:

    Explore the website to learn more about the hackathon, its theme, and any specific rules or guidelines. Make sure it's the hackathon you want to participate in.

  3. Begin Registration:

    Once you're on the registration page, you'll see the "Hackathon Registration" form. This is where you'll provide your details.

  4. Fill Out the Form:

    Complete the registration form by entering the following information:

    • Email: Enter your email address. Make sure it's a valid and active email that you can access.
    • Create Password: Choose a secure password for your account.
    • Name: Enter your full name.
    • College: Specify the name of your college or educational institution.
    • Date of Birth: Select your date of birth from the provided calendar.
    • Upload Problem Statement: Upload a PDF document containing your problem statement. Make sure it follows the specified format or guidelines.

  5. Review Your Information:

    Double-check all the information you have entered in the form to ensure accuracy.

  6. Submit the Form:

    Click the "Register" button at the bottom of the form to submit your registration.

  7. Confirmation:

    After submitting the form, you may receive a confirmation message on the website indicating that your registration was successful.

  8. Check Your Email:

    Keep an eye on your email inbox. You might receive a confirmation email from the hackathon organizers. This email may contain important details about the event.

  9. Prepare for the Hackathon:

    Now that you're registered, start preparing for the hackathon. Familiarize yourself with the event schedule, gather any necessary tools or resources, and be ready to participate on the specified date and time.

  10. Need Help?

    If you have any questions or encounter issues during the registration process, you can click on the "Contact Us" link provided on the page to reach out to the hackathon organizers for assistance.
